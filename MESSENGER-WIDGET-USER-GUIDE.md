# Messenger Widget - User Guide

## Overview
The Messenger Widget provides a Facebook-style floating chat interface that stays accessible at the bottom of your screen as you browse the site. It allows you to have multiple conversations open simultaneously without leaving your current page.

---

## Getting Started

### Opening the Messenger
1. Look for the **blue circular button** in the bottom-right (or bottom-left) corner of your screen
2. Click the button to reveal your active conversations

### Understanding the Interface
- **Main Button**: The large circular button shows a messages icon and displays the total number of unread messages as a red badge
- **Chat Circles**: When opened, you'll see circular avatars stacked vertically representing your active conversations
- **Unread Badges**: Red numbered badges on avatars indicate unread messages in that conversation

---

## Managing Conversations

### Opening a Chat Window
1. Click on any **chat circle/avatar** to open that conversation
2. The chat window will appear to the left of the messenger button
3. You can have **multiple chat windows** open at once (default maximum: 3)

### Chat Window Components
Each chat window includes:
- **Header**: Shows the contact's avatar and name with a blue background
- **Message Area**: Displays your conversation history
- **Input Area**: Where you type and send messages

### Window Controls
In the chat header, you'll find two buttons:
- **Minimize (−)**: Collapse the chat to just show the header bar
- **Close (×)**: Close the chat window completely

---

## Sending Messages

### Text Messages
1. Click in the **text input area** at the bottom of the chat window
2. Type your message
3. Press **Enter** or click the **blue send button** to send
4. Your message appears on the right side with a blue background

### Uploading Files/Images
1. Click the **paperclip/upload icon** next to the text input
2. Select an image from your device
3. The image will be sent immediately in the chat
4. Click on any image in the conversation to view it full-screen

---

## Message Features

### Message Status
- **Time Stamp**: Each message shows the time it was sent below the message bubble
- **Seen Indicator**: Your sent messages show "Seen" when the recipient has viewed them
- **Message Types**:
  - **Sent messages**: Blue background, aligned to the right
  - **Received messages**: Gray background, aligned to the left

### Reading Messages
- **Scroll** through the message history using your mouse wheel or trackpad
- The chat automatically scrolls to show the newest messages
- Unread badge numbers update automatically as you read messages

---

## Tooltips & Previews

### Chat Preview Tooltips
When you hover over a chat circle, a **tooltip appears** showing:
- Contact name
- Preview of the last message
- Positioned to the side of the avatar for easy reading

---

## Multiple Conversations

### Managing Multiple Chats
- Open **up to 3 chat windows** at the same time (or as configured by the site)
- Chat windows stack **side by side** to the left of the main messenger button
- Each chat operates independently
- You can **minimize** conversations you want to keep accessible without cluttering your screen

### Switching Between Chats
1. Click the **messenger button** to view all your active conversations
2. Click any **chat circle** to open or bring that conversation to focus
3. Already-open chats can be brought to the front by clicking their header

---

## Navigation & Browsing

### Staying Connected
- The messenger widget **stays visible** as you navigate between pages
- Your chat windows **remain open** as you browse
- **Unread message badges update** in real-time
- You can continue conversations without interruption

### Closing the Chat List
- Click the **messenger button** again to hide the chat circles
- Click anywhere **outside** the messenger area to close the chat list
- Your open chat windows remain visible

---

## Notifications

### Unread Message Badges
- **Red numbered badges** appear on:
  - The main messenger button (total unread messages)
  - Individual chat circles (unread per conversation)
- Badges **automatically disappear** when you view the messages
- Badge numbers **update in real-time** as new messages arrive

---

## Image Viewing

### Lightbox Gallery
When viewing images in your conversations:
1. **Click any image** to view it full-screen
2. The image opens in a **lightbox overlay** with a dark background
3. **Click the X** in the top-right corner to close
4. **Click outside** the image to close the lightbox

---

## Tips & Best Practices

### Efficient Messaging
- Use **Enter** to send messages quickly without clicking the send button
- **Minimize chats** you want to keep open but not actively viewing
- **Close chats** you're done with to reduce screen clutter
- Open the **chat list** frequently to see all your active conversations at a glance

### Privacy & Availability
- The messenger only appears when you're **logged in** to the site
- Your conversations are **private** and only visible to you and the other participant
- The messenger may be **hidden on certain pages** (like checkout) as configured by the site

### Mobile Usage
- On smaller screens, the messenger **adapts automatically**
- Chat windows become **full-screen** on very small mobile devices
- You may be limited to **one chat at a time** on mobile
- The interface remains **touch-friendly** with appropriately sized buttons

---

## Accessibility

### Keyboard Navigation
- Use **Tab** to navigate between chat windows and controls
- Press **Enter** in the text input to send messages
- **Escape key** closes lightbox image views

### Screen Readers
- All buttons include **descriptive labels**
- Chat structure is **semantically organized**
- Image uploads include **alternative text**

---

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Messages not sending:**
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure the text input is not empty
- Try refreshing the page

**Chat windows not opening:**
- Click directly on the avatar circle
- Ensure you haven't reached the maximum number of open chats
- Try closing an existing chat first

**Images not uploading:**
- Check your file size (very large images may not upload)
- Ensure the file is a supported image format (JPG, PNG, etc.)
- Verify your internet connection

**Unread badges not updating:**
- Ensure you've scrolled to view all messages in the conversation
- Try refreshing the page
- Check your internet connection

## Privacy & Data

### What's Stored
- Your **message history** is saved for future reference
- **Upload images** are stored securely
- **Conversation state** (open/closed, read/unread) persists across page loads

### Your Data Rights
- You can **delete conversations** through your account settings
- **Message data** belongs to you and the other conversation participant
- Follow the site's privacy policy for more information about data handling

3. Try a **different browser** to isolate the issue
